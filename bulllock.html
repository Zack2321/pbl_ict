Myanmar Bullock Cart Racing

The bullock cart and bullock race is being held as part of the Buddha Pujaniya Tabaung festival, which is traditionally held every year. The traditional race was not held for about 30 years. So, the present race was organized with the support of the Magway Region government and officials from township departments.
