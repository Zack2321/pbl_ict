Myanmar Boat Racing

According to the ancient Rakhine history chronicle, Rakhine style boat races were celebrated first during the reign of the Rakhine (Arfakan) king. Currently, the boat race is held traditionally and annually as a Rakhine sport. To celebrate it, it is essential to know the time of rising tide and ebb tide.
